Privacy & Opt‑out

Last updated 2025-08-17

What we collect

  • Beta waitlist & contact: your name, email, and message (if provided) via our contact forms.
  • New Seller Database: business information available on public pages/directories (e.g., store names, URLs, categories). Where available, we capture a public contact route (email, contact form, or social profile).
  • Analytics: anonymous usage metrics via Google Analytics 4.

How we use data

  • To invite you to the beta and send samples.
  • To improve the database (quality, freshness, coverage).
  • To keep our site secure and working as expected.

Provenance & freshness

Each database row includes source_url, captured_at, and last_checked_at fields. We aim to keep ≥80% of rows updated within 30 days.

Opt‑out

If your business appears in our database and you’d like it removed or corrected, email us at YOUR_EMAIL_HERE. We’ll confirm and process within 5 business days.

Third‑party services

  • Form handling via FormSubmit.
  • Analytics via Google Analytics 4.
  • Hosting via GitHub Pages.
